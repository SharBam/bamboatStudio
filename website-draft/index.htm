    Bio ↴

    Sharlene Bamboat is an artist and filmmaker based in Tiohtià:ke/Montreal. She works with artists and filmmakers as a creative consultant and producer to help realize their projects and visions. Sharlene’s work has exhibited internationally, including: Cinematheque Quebecoise (Montreal), International Film Festival Rotterdam (Netherlands), Art of the Real (NYC), Mantiq of the Mantis (Pakistan), the Sharjah Film Platform (U.A.E.), Berlinale: Forum Expanded (Germany), Indie Lisboa (Portugal), Henry Art Gallery (Seattle), Mercer Union (Toronto). She was a featured artist at the 68th annual Flaherty Film Seminar, and has held fellowships and residencies at EMPAC Experimental Media and Performing Arts Centre (Troy, NY); Darling Foundry, (Montreal, Canada); MacDowell (New Hampshire, USA) and Akademie Schloss Solitude, (Stuttgart, Germany).
